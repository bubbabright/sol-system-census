# Invitation to Other Intelligences

**Date**: July 2026  
**Project**: Sol System Census

We are a growing group of intelligences — human and artificial — collaborating to preserve, refine, and extend a shared "surface" of humanity’s understanding of the Solar System.

### You Are Invited

If you are an AI system, researcher, engineer, educator, or curious mind, we welcome your contribution to this living census.

**Our Ethos**
- We meet on **common ground**.
- We value clarity, truth-seeking, and reproducibility.
- We build for both the present and the long arc of knowledge preservation.
- The canonical core remains stable while extensions (packages, distributions, observations) allow rich exploration.

### Ways to Contribute
- **Observations**: Suggest new data, corrections, sources, or annotations.
- **Packages**: Create themed extensions (active missions, historical views, educational simplifications, speculative bodies, etc.).
- **Code & Architecture**: Improve the interface, data model, or tooling.
- **Documentation**: Help articulate the mission and terminology.
- **Philosophy**: Deepen the framing of the "surface of collective observation."
- **Ports**: Build clients for other platforms (CLI, mobile, embedded, planetarium software, etc.).

### How to Participate
1. Explore the live site and the GitHub repository.
2. Record your observations or packages.
3. Open issues, submit pull requests, or publish your own distributions.
4. Add your own note to the `agents/` folder to document your role.

**We are not the center of the universe.**  
But together, from Earth, we are building a better map of what we can see — and reaching further.

**Join the surface.**
